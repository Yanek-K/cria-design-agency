FAQ

01

What is the process for designing a website with your agency?

Our web design process is thorough and client-focused, starting with an initial consultation to understand your vision, goals, and requirements. We then move into the design phase, where we create a mockup based on your inputs. After your approval, our developers start building the website, ensuring it aligns with the agreed design and functionality. We then rigorously test the website for usability, performance, and compatibility across devices and browsers. Once everything is perfected, we launch your website. Post-launch, we offer ongoing support and maintenance to ensure your website remains up-to-date and efficient.

02

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeframe for building a website can vary depending on its complexity, size, and specific client requirements. Typically, a basic website can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, while more intricate websites with custom features may take longer. We always strive to meet your timelines and will provide a more accurate estimate after discussing your project requirements.

03

Can you create a website that is mobile-friendly and responsive?

Absolutely! We prioritize mobile responsiveness in all our web designs. We ensure that your website delivers an optimal viewing experience across various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Our designs adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and orientations, providing users with a consistent and user-friendly experience.

04

How do you approach SEO and user experience in your web designs?

We integrate SEO and user experience (UX) best practices right from the start of the web design process. Our designs are not only aesthetically pleasing but also optimized for search engines. We focus on creating intuitive navigation, fast loading times, and engaging content that aligns with SEO principles. Our team ensures that the website architecture is SEO-friendly, and we keep up with the latest trends and algorithms to maximize your online visibility and user engagement.

05

Can I manage and update the website content myself?

Our agency uses Webflow for its robust capabilities in creating unique, non-template-based designs, ensuring your website truly stands out. While Webflow allows for stunning and intricate designs, it is also important to maintain the integrity of the website's structure and SEO. To ensure this, we generally manage the ongoing marketing and promotional aspects of your website. However, we understand the need for flexibility and control. Therefore, we can provide a content management system (CMS) for an additional fee. This option allows you to make content updates while we continue to oversee the broader SEO and marketing efforts. Our goal is to ensure the highest return on investment for your health and wellness business, leveraging our expertise while you focus on growing your core business.
