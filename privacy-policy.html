Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2024

1. Introduction

At Cria Design Agency, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage with our digital marketing services.

By accessing or using our website and services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please take the time to read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide to us voluntarily. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide when you fill out contact forms or request information from us.

b. Usage Information: We may collect information about your interactions with our website, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the duration of your visit. This data helps us improve our website and services.

c. Cookies: Like many websites, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device. We use cookies to collect information about your preferences and track your usage patterns on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

a. To provide and maintain our website and services.

b. To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

c. To send you marketing and promotional communications if you have opted in to receive them.

d. To analyze website usage and improve our content and services.

e. To protect our website and services from fraudulent or illegal activities.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to the following:

a. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing our services. These service providers have access to your personal information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

b. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information when required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or similar legal process.

5. Your Choices

a. Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving marketing and promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the emails or by contacting us directly.

b. Cookies: Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually adjust your browser settings to disable cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post any changes to this Privacy Policy on our website, and the revised policy will be effective as of the "Effective Date" stated at the beginning of this Privacy Policy.
